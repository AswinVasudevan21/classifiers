Angle Annotation

Objective: The below image contains 4 angles in it from left to right. The approximate estimation of each angle should be entered in the textbox below. The protractor scale given to the left can used as measure for estimating the angle.


Instruction:

1. The below image contains 4 angles in it from left to right.
2. From left estimate the angle of the first one and enter the value in first box.
3. Similarly complete all 4 angles in each image.
4. Then move to the next image and continue the same.
5. After completing all the images hit submit.
